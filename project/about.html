About Bangladesh Biman

Biman Bangladesh Airlines, the national carrier of Bangladesh, was founded in 1972, shortly after the country's independence. It operates both domestic and international flights, connecting Bangladesh to major destinations across Asia, Europe, and the Middle East. With its headquarters in Dhaka, Biman has a fleet that includes a mix of modern aircraft, primarily serving travelers with a focus on comfort, safety, and reliability. Over the years, the airline has played a key role in promoting tourism, trade, and cultural exchange, while continuously working on expanding its network and improving service standards.
