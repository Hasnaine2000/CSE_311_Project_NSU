Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I book a flight?
To book a flight, simply go to the 'Search Flight' section, enter your travel details (departure and arrival cities, dates, and passenger info), and choose from the available flights. Follow the prompts to complete your booking.
2. Can I change my flight after booking?
Yes, most airlines offer the option to change your flight after booking. However, this may involve additional fees or fare differences, depending on the airline's policies. Check with your airline for specific terms.
3. What is the baggage policy?
Baggage policies vary by airline, but most airlines allow one carry-on bag and one checked bag. Additional fees may apply for extra baggage, oversized items, or special luggage. Refer to the airline's baggage policy before traveling.
4. How can I cancel my flight?
You can cancel your flight by contacting the airline directly or through their website. Be aware that cancellations are subject to the airline's cancellation policy, and fees may apply based on the timing and ticket type.
